## Who is IVOR?

### The Historical Ivor Cummings
IVOR is inspired by **Ivor Cummings** (1916-1991), a pioneering Black gay civil servant who became legendary for his ability to connect people and resources. Working in the Home Office during a time when both his race and sexuality could have ended his career, Ivor built an incredible network of contacts that he used to help others navigate systems and find support.

Ivor was known for:
- **Knowing everyone**: He seemed to know someone who could help with any problem
- **Generous spirit**: Always willing to share his network and knowledge
- **Cultural wisdom**: Understanding how to navigate white institutions while preserving Black identity
- **Community care**: Quietly supporting people through difficult times

### Our Digital IVOR
Our AI assistant carries forward this legacy, designed to:
- **Connect you with resources**: Find the right support for your specific needs
- **Share community wisdom**: Provide culturally relevant guidance and information
- **Bridge organizations**: Help you discover services across multiple organizations
- **Preserve knowledge**: Keep community wisdom accessible for future generations

## How IVOR Helps You

### Instant Support
- **Ask any question**: About housing, healthcare, legal rights, mental health, social groups
- **Get immediate answers**: No waiting for office hours or callback
- **Find local resources**: Services specifically in your area
- **Connect with community**: Discover events, groups, and activities

### Examples of What You Can Ask IVOR
- "I'm struggling with my mental health and need someone who understands being Black and queer"
- "Are there any trans-friendly GPs in South London?"
- "What events are happening this weekend for queer people in Birmingham?"
- "I need help with housing benefits but don't know where to start"
- "Can you help me find a Black therapist who works with young people?"

### Cultural Understanding
IVOR is designed to understand:
- **Intersectional identities**: Being Black AND queer, not just one or the other
- **Community language**: The way we actually talk, not formal institutional language
- **Cultural context**: Understanding family dynamics, community pressures, and systemic barriers
- **Safe spaces**: Knowing which services are truly welcoming and culturally competent

## Your Privacy and Data

### What We Do
- **Answer your questions**: Using publicly available community resource information
- **Connect you with services**: Provide contact information and guidance
- **Learn from interactions**: Improve responses based on what questions people ask
- **Protect your identity**: No personal information stored or shared

### What We Don't Do
- **Store personal details**: No names, addresses, or personal information kept
- **Share your conversations**: What you ask IVOR stays private
- **Judge or discriminate**: Designed to be welcoming to all Black queer identities
- **Replace human connection**: Complement, not replace, real community relationships

### Transparency Promise
- **Open development**: You can see how we're building IVOR
- **Community control**: Community feedback shapes how IVOR develops
- **Clear policies**: Transparent about data use and privacy protection
- **Right to feedback**: Your input directly influences IVOR's improvement

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is IVOR free to use?
Yes, IVOR is completely free for all community members and always will be.

### Will IVOR replace human support?
No, IVOR is designed to complement human support by helping you find the right people and services more quickly.

### How do I know IVOR's information is accurate?
IVOR's knowledge comes from trusted community organizations and is regularly updated. You can always verify information by contacting services directly.

### What if IVOR doesn't understand my question?
IVOR is learning! If something doesn't work, please let us know and we'll improve it.

### Can I use IVOR if I'm not "out" or don't use labels?
Absolutely. IVOR is designed to help anyone seeking support, regardless of how you identify or what language you use.

### Will IVOR work on my phone?
Yes, IVOR is designed to work on all devices, including older phones and slow internet connections.
