## Executive Summary

IVOR (Informed Voice Of Resources) is a revolutionary community intelligence platform that will transform how Black queer organizations collaborate and serve our community. Inspired by Ivor Cummings' legacy of community networking, this platform demonstrates our collective power while building sustainable infrastructure for our future.

### Why We Need IVOR
- **Fragmented Resources**: Community members struggle to find relevant support across multiple organizations
- **Duplicate Efforts**: Organizations independently maintain similar resource lists and event calendars
- **Hidden Connections**: Potential partnerships and collaborations go undiscovered
- **Knowledge Loss**: Community wisdom and cultural context risk being lost without preservation

### The IVOR Solution
- **Unified Access**: Single point of entry for all community resources and events
- **Collaborative Efficiency**: Shared infrastructure reducing individual organizational burden
- **Intelligence Layer**: AI-powered insights identifying partnership opportunities and community needs
- **Cultural Preservation**: Digital embodiment of Ivor Cummings' community networking legacy
