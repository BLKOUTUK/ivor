- Clean, modern design with BLKOUT branding
- Navigation menu: Home, Events, Resources, About
- Hero section with community messaging
- **IVOR chat button** (fixed, bottom-right): "💬 Ask IVOR"

### 4. **Visual Design Elements**
- **Color Scheme**: BLKOUT brand colors (deep blues, warm oranges)
- **Typography**: Clean, readable fonts with proper contrast
- **Mobile-First**: Responsive design works on all devices
- **Accessibility**: High contrast, screen reader compatible
- **Loading States**: "⏳ IVOR is thinking..." with smooth animations

### 5. **Community Values Integration**
- Footer text: "Built with cooperative ownership principles"
- Response tone: Warm, culturally authentic, not corporate
- Privacy notice: "No personal data stored"
- Source attribution: Transparent about information sources

## User Journey Flow

1. **Land on BLKOUT website**
2. **Notice unobtrusive IVOR chat button**
3. **Click to open chat window**
4. **Read welcoming introduction**
5. **Type question about community need**
6. **See loading indicator**
7. **Receive culturally authentic response**
8. **Get specific organization contacts**
9. **Can continue conversation or close**

This visual guide shows exactly what users experience when interacting with IVOR - a seamless blend of technology and community values that provides authentic, helpful support while honoring Black queer experiences and collective liberation principles.
